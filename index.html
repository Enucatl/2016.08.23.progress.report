KIT gratings from Maria

  • various grating sets for 30 and 45 \(\pi\) shift
  • short summary: gratings are ok, homogeneity and absorption thickness are the main concerns.
  • \(\lt 100\) μm gold forces the smaller focal spot (1 mm)
  • visibility 8-10% for most sets
  • best configuration averaging 15%
